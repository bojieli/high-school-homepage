The wind is blowing like a flow
as the time of maths go
The problem is too hard to get through
while anything is possible to do so
The prize is just one goal
but nothing is under my control
Life is sometimes a sail
that no one can do any steal
I stand so still
listening whatever you feel
　
By Li Bojie    Nov.27, 2008
